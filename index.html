Spaghetti Carbonara
A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.
Ingredients:
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g Parmesan
  • Black pepper
Preparation:

Cook the pasta, fry the pancetta, mix eggs with cheese, then combine all with pasta over low heat.

Avocado Quinoa Salad
A refreshing and healthy mix of quinoa, avocado, and fresh veggies.
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 avocado
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Lemon juice
Preparation:

Cook quinoa, chop veggies and mix with lemon juice and avocado. Serve chilled.
